Stock Back Date

The Standard Vanilla Odoo does not Support Backdating Transactions. However, with the Introduction of This Module, Users Gain the Flexibility to Modify the Scheduled Date to a Past Date. Consequently, All Associated Documents, Such as Stock Movements and Journal Entries Linked to Deliveries or Shipments, Will Reflect the Selected Date. This Enhancement Allows for Greater Adaptability in Managing Transactional Timelines Within the Odoo System.



Features

Confirm Delivery / Shipment with a Backdate.

All Related Document such as Stock Move, Move Line, Journal Entry are Reflect the Selected Date.

Multi company Support.

No More Configuration.

How Application Work



Enable Allow Stock Back Date


Set Value Of Date Scheduled and Deadline From Scheduled Date While Validating Stock Order


Updated Date In Stock Valuation


Updated Date In Stock Move


Updated Date In Journal Entries Of Product
